Ready to launch your online store? or Website?

You're an entrepreneur with a product to sell, now all you need is a beautiful online store.

As Shopify Partner, I'm well equipped to create and launch your e-commerce store, no matter the size of your business.

From setting up your Shopify store all the way to launch, I will take care of everything. Reach out, tell me about your project and in a few weeks we'll be celebrating your first order!

I make professional Shopify stores

  • Beautiful themes

    Your online store's appearance can have a big impact on sales. Shopify Theme Store includes over 100 free and premium professionally designed ecommerce website templates that you can use for your own online store.

  • Manage your products

    Manage your entire inventory with Shopify. Organize your products by category, type, season, sale and more. Add multiple images for your products, so you can show it off from all angles.

  • Your own domain name

    A domain is the URL, or the website address, where your customers go to find your store online. Shopify offers the possibility to add your own domain name to make it easier for your customers to find your store.

  • Orders and payments

    Accept credit cards and other payment methods to make checkout convenient for customers. Payment details are clearly synced with orders, making it easy to see how much you’ve been paid without ever leaving Shopify.

  • Shipping

    Before you take your first order, you need to decide what shipping methods you want to use, and then set up your store's shipping settings so that your customers can choose a delivery method at checkout.

  • Marketing and discounts

    After you launch your store, it's important to increase traffic and convert visitors into customers. Developing a marketing plan can help you to choose marketing tactics that are right for your store.
